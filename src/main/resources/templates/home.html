About the issue


Abusive circumstances, instances of transphobia, and inability to access formal work opportunities are just some of the reasons why trans people face homelessness at higher rates than other groups, which leaves them in an even more vulnerable situation. This is unacceptable. The law won't protect them, but we can. We aim to build a community, provide safe spaces, support systems and, above all, create opportunities, because trans people deserve the right to thrive.

Only 40% of Peruvians would hire a trans person.


According to Universidad Peruana Cayetano Heredia, only 3% of this community can access a formal job. Due to the pandemic, their situation has become even more precarious.
